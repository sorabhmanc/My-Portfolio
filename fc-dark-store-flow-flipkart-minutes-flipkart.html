FC → Dark Store Flow (Flipkart Minutes) (Flipkart)

Flipkart • Program Manager — Linehaul & Network Design

Dock Scheduling Routing Workforce Planning TMS

Replenishment often waited on the wrong prerequisite—either a wave wasn’t closed or a dock wasn’t free. We wired the plan end‑to‑end: FC waves publish ready times, a slotter books docks accordingly, staffing is scheduled to cover those slots, and the router assigns trips when both conditions are green.

Exceptions show up in a single queue for ops to fix quickly. The effect was fewer last‑minute scrambles and better stock availability at the dark stores.

What changed

  • Smoother dock flow
  • Fewer late departures
